 # COVID-19 data
 Information about Covid is collected in a standalone Covid survey and in the Follow-up Medical Survey in the Human Phenotype Project study.

 This Covid survey presents the participants with questions regarding their Covid-19 vaccinations and diagnosis status. It is asked in-person, in every visit to the clinical testing center.
